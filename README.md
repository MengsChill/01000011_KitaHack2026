# POLE, smart walking stick
POLE redefines what a walking aid can do. Beyond its stylish exterior, this advanced device is packed with cutting-edge technology to elevate your daily journeys. Stay connected and informed with real-time data on steps, distance, and even weather updates. The integrated GPS location tracker ensures that you never lose track of your walking cane and helps your loved ones to get in touch with you. Additionally, the object detection feature enhances safety by alerting you to obstacles ahead. Powered by a rechargeable 9V battery, our product can function for up to 30 hours after an 8-hour charge, allowing users to take longer walks without having to charge their device frequently. Experience a new level of convenience, security, and connectedness with POLE.
